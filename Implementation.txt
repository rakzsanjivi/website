Portfolio - MNC-Grade Redesign Implementation Plan

This plan details the steps to fix all layout flaws and redesign the portfolio
to a production-level standard that meets expectations of top MNC recruiters.

IDENTIFIED FLAWS FROM CURRENT SCREENSHOT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FLAW 1 — NAVBAR LOGO BREAKING INTO 3 LINES
The logo "RAGHAVARAJAN S U." is overflowing and
stacking vertically (3 separate lines visible in
screenshot). Completely breaks navbar layout.

FLAW 2 — HERO TITLE OVERFLOW ON MOBILE
"Developer Portfolio" heading is 80px font size
and exceeds viewport width on laptop/tablet.
No clamp() or responsive font scaling applied.

FLAW 3 — HERO SECTION TOP OVERLAP
The hero content starts too high and overlaps
with the fixed navbar. Insufficient padding-top
to account for floating navbar height.

FLAW 4 — ROLE CARDS CUT OFF AT BOTTOM
The 4 role cards (Data Analyst, Python Dev,
Power BI Expert, SQL Specialist) are partially
cut off below the viewport fold with no scroll
indicator or breathing room beneath them.

FLAW 5 — MISSING SKILLS SECTION CONTENT
Skills nav link exists but the section has no
visible content rendered in the browser.

FLAW 6 — NO MOBILE HAMBURGER MENU
Nav links disappear on mobile (display:none)
with no hamburger replacement. Site is
unusable on phone screens.

FLAW 7 — WEAK VISUAL HIERARCHY
All text is the same white/grey. No accent
color used to create visual focus points.
MNC portfolio needs a signature accent.

FLAW 8 — ROLE CARDS NOT EQUAL WIDTH
Cards in the role-map row have unequal widths,
making the connected line layout look broken
and unprofessional.
